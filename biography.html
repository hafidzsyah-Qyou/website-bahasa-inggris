Ir. Soekarno

Biography Text Quiz

Ir. Soekarno was the first President of Indonesia. He was born on June 6, 1901, in Surabaya. Soekarno was an important figure in Indonesia’s independence movement. Together with Mohammad Hatta, he proclaimed Indonesian independence on August 17, 1945. He became a symbol of nationalism and inspired many Indonesians to fight for freedom. Ir. Soekarno passed away on June 21, 1970, but his legacy remains important in Indonesian history.
