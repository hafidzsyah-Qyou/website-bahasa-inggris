The Lion King

Narrative Text Quiz

Once upon a time, there was a brave lion who ruled the jungle wisely. All animals respected him because he was strong and kind. One day, hunters came into the jungle and trapped the lion using strong ropes. The lion roared loudly for help. A small mouse heard the roar and quickly came. The mouse bit the ropes with its sharp teeth and finally freed the lion. The lion thanked the mouse and learned that even a small creature can help greatly.
