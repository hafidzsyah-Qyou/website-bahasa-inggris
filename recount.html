First Day at School

Recount Text Quiz

Last Monday was my first day at school. I woke up very early and prepared my school uniform. I felt nervous because I would meet many new friends and teachers. When I arrived at school, the students and teachers welcomed me warmly. We introduced ourselves in class and learned about school rules. At the end of the day, I felt happy and excited to return to school again.
